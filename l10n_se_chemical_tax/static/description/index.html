Chemical tax

Chemical tax For companies that must report chemical tax to the Swedish Tax Agency.

Get a chemical tax report in Sales report view.

Depends on modules sale, product, stock and l10n_se
Add fields for chemical tax and max tax in product category.


          Installation

                     This module needs these modules to work:
          l10n_se: Sweden - Accounting (l10n_se)

          Install module l10n_se_chemical_tax
          Go to Sales and under the menu select Products/Product variants
          Click on a product and then in the form view for the product click on Product category
          Here you will find fields for chemical tax at the bottom of the form view.
          Click Edit and fill in the current tax rates from the Swedish Tax Agency.

          Usage

                     The report can be viewed in the pivot view for Sales -> Sales analysis.
          Select Chemical Tax as Measure
